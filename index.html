Diabetes in the US from 2000 to 2021

Diabetes has been a critical health issue that has impacted society throughout the years. One of the most pressing concerns facing the United States over the past two decades has been the rise in diabetes cases. From 2000 to 2021, this once-considered "silent epidemic" has ballooned into a significant public health challenge, affecting millions of individuals across the nation.

Methodology

Background and Motivation:
Both my parents are pre-diabetic and a couple friends that are also Type 1 Diabetic. Thus I wanted to do some more in depth research on it and see how it is affecting other people in the US. I also recently interviewed for a company that does CGM (continuous glucose monitoring) and I really liked their project goals and the people that I talked to who worked there.

I used a multitude of different resources. My dataset is from the CDC and I cleaned it using Excel and Tableau.
